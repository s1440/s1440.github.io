About This Portfolio

This site showcases my work from CIT 281 at the University of Oregon. It includes a collection of lab exercises and major projects completed throughout the course, covering topics like JavaScript, Node.js, Express, APIs, and GitHub.

Each lab and project demonstrates practical coding skills and deeper understanding of client-server architecture, programming logic, and version control. The portfolio also serves as a personal milestone in my journey through the Computer & Information Technology minor.
